# user-research in the Open Home Foundation

This repository is the home of User Research within the Open Home Foundation and serves as the central place for research collaboration and direction. It is fully open and welcomes contributions from researchers.

### Purpose

- Make Research work transparent
- Enable community researchers to contribute their findings
- Document research findings openly

### How research work is organized

- The [Project Board](https://github.com/OpenHomeFoundation/user-research/projects) contains all active and planned research tasks
